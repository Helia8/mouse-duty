# mouse-duty

Game is set in an arctic base.
You're a scientist tasked with retrieving and sending various meteorological data
Everyday you wake up in your arctic base and try to accomplish your duties
However, you must also deal with the maintenance of your base
Every day in game, at random, you'll one or many random events that will
hinder both your work and survival.

Your goal is to make it to keep for say 30 days, until you get switched out (ending)
You may or may not go outside to do some content (fix stuff, get food whatever)
Each device in your base can be broken/repaired and offer special abilities
for example, heating would allow you to not die inside your base, and
be hot enough so you can explore outside a little bit.
Could add mechanics tied to food (limited food supply for the month, with events where you could lose some))
You also have like an energy bar, which represent your base's total energy supply
it slowly decreases every day, and when you use certain devices.
 
The only way to replenish it is to complete your daily tasks
